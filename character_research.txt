Character Development Research for Modern Romance

Key Principles:
1. Flawed protagonists create relatability
2. Internal conflict drives external action  
3. Character growth through meaningful challenges
4. Authentic dialogue reveals personality
5. Visual actions show character traits

Sarah represents the modern professional struggling with work-life balance and trust issues stemming from a previous business partnership betrayal. Her character arc involves learning to open up and trust others again.

Marcus embodies the creative spirit who values human connection over material success. His coffee shop serves as a sanctuary for artists and dreamers, contrasting with Sarah's high-pressure tech world.

Dr. Elena provides wisdom and guidance, representing the mentor archetype who helps others navigate life transitions through psychological insight and emotional intelligence.
