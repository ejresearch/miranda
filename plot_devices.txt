Romantic Comedy Plot Devices

The Meet-Cute:
Sarah spills coffee on her laptop. Marcus offers to fix it, revealing unexpected technical skills that bridge their different worlds.

The Misunderstanding:
Sarah overhears Marcus talking about "the deal" (actually planning a surprise poetry reading) and assumes he's involved in corporate espionage.

The Mentor's Wisdom:
Elena uses gardening metaphors to teach Sarah about patience, growth, and nurturing relationships.

The Competition:
Jake's startup directly competes with Sarah's, forcing her to choose between ruthless success and ethical business practices.

The Grand Gesture:
Marcus organizes a public poetry reading where he performs a piece about Sarah, risking his reputation to show his feelings.
